warranty

Just to give you that peace of mind we are fully licensed and fully insured.

Farran-Heit air conditioning offer a 5 years domestic installation warranty & 5 years manufacture's warranty when you purchase a airconditioner from farran-heit, you have the satisfaction of knowing you've purchased a quality product. All farran-heit air conditioning residential air conditioners (excludes commercial), carry a five year parts and labour warranty. An additional 5 years installation warranty available upon an annual service of your system

Farran-Heit can help you if a warranty issue arises (if not installation fault). If the unit is faulty the unit will be covered under the manufacturer warranty the manufacturer details will be on the warranty form which came with the unit purchased. When placing a warranty claim you will need to provide proof of purchase (your farran-heit air conditioning invoice).
